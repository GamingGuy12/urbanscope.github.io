Platform Overview

Connecting Citizens to Municipal Services

UrbanScope is Yangon's civic infrastructure monitoring platform, enabling residents to report issues, track engineering responses, and stay informed about ongoing maintenance operations across the city.

From flooding alerts to road damage reports, every submission is routed directly to the relevant municipal authority for action and transparent follow-up.

About This Project

Built by TRES R&D

UrbanScope was developed by TRES R&D as part of a project-based initiative to bridge the gap between Yangon residents and the city's public infrastructure teams.

Our data is sourced from citizen submissions, engineering field teams, and municipal databases, aggregated into a single transparent dashboard that anyone can access and contribute to.

How to File a Report

Your Report, Our Action

Submitting a report takes less than two minutes. Select the issue type, provide a location, optionally attach photo evidence, and submit. You'll receive a unique tracking ID.

Use that ID at any time on the Track My Report page to follow live engineering updates.
