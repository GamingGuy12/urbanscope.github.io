Inya Lake Park Jogging Circuit - Surface Cracking

fitness_ygnr
Maintenance

The paved northern jogging circuit around Inya Lake Park has developed significant longitudinal cracks spanning approximately 80 metres near the university-side entrance. Several sections have lifted and are a tripping hazard, especially in poor lighting conditions. A report has been filed (USB-2026-2966) but the path remains open without any hazard markers.

Botataung Riverside Promenade - Night Lighting Insufficient

riverside_walker
Lighting

The riverside promenade between the Botataung Pagoda jetty and the Strand Road overpass has only intermittent lighting, with roughly 60% of lamp posts either non-functional or absent. Evening foot traffic along this stretch is significant, and the lack of visibility creates personal safety concerns - particularly for women walking alone.
